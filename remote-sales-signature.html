Remote Sales Signature Project

Home Remote Sales Signature

This project involved adapting the sales process to remote operations during the pandemic by implementing an electronic signature solution compliant with the Electronic Signature Laws & Regulations, capturing biometric data, and improving the overall signature process.

Vendor Selection and Solution Implementation

Contacted and evaluated multiple remote solution providers, attending demos to assess features and compliance.

Selected a solution compliant with Electronic Signature Laws & Regulations in place, which captured biometric data, including pressure, angle, and velocity, for secure signatures.

Collaboration with Cross-Functional Teams

Coordinated with the solutions architect and infrastructure team to procure and install hardware necessary to support the new electronic signature module.

Collaborated with various internal teams to ensure policy documents were updated with the necessary changes to accommodate digital signatures.

Process Optimization

Worked closely with the legal team to explore the possibility of reducing the number of signatures required, implementing changes that affected all distribution platforms.

Achieved significant improvements by streamlining the signature process, enhancing efficiency and user experience.

Security Assurance

Conducted penetration testing (pentest) of the electronic signature functionality to ensure compliance with security standards and protect sensitive data.

Adapting to Remote Work

The pandemic created the need for a fast pivot to remote operations. By thoroughly researching and evaluating digital signature solutions, the team was able to seamlessly integrate a secure, compliant remote sales process.

Legacy System Documentation Gaps

Ensured the solution met all necessary regulatory requirements for electronic signatures, including capturing biometric data and adhering to electronic signature standards.

Outcome

Successfully implemented a secure, remote sales signature solution that allowed for the continuation of sales processes during the pandemic.

Streamlined the signature process by reducing the number of signatures required, contributing to faster sales closures and improved user satisfaction.

Ensured the system's security and compliance through thorough penetration testing.

Tools & Technologies Used
